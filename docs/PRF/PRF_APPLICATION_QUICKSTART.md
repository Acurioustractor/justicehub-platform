## Potential Challenges & Solutions

### Challenge: "I'm not sure which host organization to choose"

**Solution:**
- Approach 3 options in parallel
- Ask PRF for support (they help match fellows to hosts)
- In application, note: "In discussion with [organization names], open to PRF support for host match"

### Challenge: "I don't have all the community partnerships confirmed yet"

**Solution:**
- Name organizations you're in conversation with
- Be honest: "Early discussions with [org names], will formalize if fellowship awarded"
- Demonstrate you have relationships, even if not formal MOUs

### Challenge: "The budget feels complicated"

**Solution:**
- Use high-level categories from draft responses document
- PRF will work with you to refine budget if selected
- Focus on: your time, community compensation, installation costs, platform development

### Challenge: "I'm worried this is too ambitious"

**Solution:**
- This fellowship is MEANT for ambitious, pioneering ideas
- PRF specifically seeks to "fill knowledge gaps" and "identify new ways"
- Your ambition matches PRF's mandate
- The integration IS the innovationâ€”it's supposed to be big

### Challenge: "What if I don't get shortlisted?"

**Solution:**
- The research is valuable regardless of funding
- Consider other funders (this strategy document helps any application)
- PRF offers this biannuallyâ€”could apply in 2027
- Feedback strengthens future applications
