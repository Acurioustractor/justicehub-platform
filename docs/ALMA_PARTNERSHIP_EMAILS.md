## Email 1: Oochiumpa Aboriginal Corporation

**To**: info@oochiumpa.org.au (or appropriate contact)
**Subject**: ALMA Youth Justice Partnership - Celebrating Oochiumpa's 95% Success Rate

---

Dear Oochiumpa Team,

I'm reaching out from A Curious Tractor (ACT), a regenerative innovation studio working on ALMA - an Adaptive Learning for Meaningful Accountability system designed to support Aboriginal-led youth justice solutions across Australia.

**Why We're Reaching Out**

Oochiumpa's work is extraordinary. Your 95% reduction in offending and 72% school re-engagement rate represents the gold standard of what's possible when Aboriginal communities lead youth justice responses. We've documented your outcomes as a benchmark program in ALMA, and we believe there's an opportunity to amplify your impact.

**What ALMA Does**

ALMA is a youth justice intelligence system that:
- Documents 624 programs across Australia (134 Aboriginal Community Controlled)
- Links research outcomes to real programs (418 programs with documented outcomes)
- Creates "Intelligence Packs" for funders, policymakers, and communities
- Operates under OCAP principles (Ownership, Control, Access, Possession)
- Highlights what works: Aboriginal-led, community-controlled, on-country programs

**The Opportunity**

We'd love to explore:
1. **Data Sovereignty**: How can ALMA better represent Oochiumpa's model while respecting your data sovereignty?
2. **Outcome Tracking**: Would Oochiumpa be interested in contributing outcome data to demonstrate the power of your approach to funders nationally?
3. **Replication Support**: Could ALMA help document what makes Oochiumpa work so other communities can learn from your model?
4. **Policy Advocacy**: Can we collaborate on showing policymakers that investing in Aboriginal-led programs delivers 95% cost-effectiveness vs $3,320/day detention?

**Our Commitment**

- All Oochiumpa data remains under your control
- Nothing is shared without explicit consent
- We operate under "Community Controlled" consent model
- Revenue from any intelligence products would be shared with contributing communities

**Next Steps**

Would you be open to a 30-minute call in the next two weeks to discuss how we might work together? I'm also happy to visit if that's more appropriate.

With respect for your leadership,
