## 1. Oochiumpa (Aboriginal-Owned Benchmark)

### Program Overview
- **Name**: Oochiumpa Youth Services
- **Type**: Wraparound Support
- **Cultural Authority**: Aboriginal Community Controlled Organisation, Central Australia
- **Evidence Level**: Indigenous-led (culturally grounded, community authority)
- **Consent**: Community Controlled (10% revenue share)

### Proven Outcomes (July-December 2024, n=21)
1. **95% offending reduction** - 18 of 19 young people removed from Operation Luna list
2. **72% school re-engagement** - From 95% baseline disengagement
3. **89% program retention** - Cultural safety drives participation
4. **71 service referrals** - Wraparound support connections
5. **10 external requests** - Community recognizes what works

### Model Characteristics
- **Aboriginal-owned** and operated
- **On-country experiences** - Cultural connection healing
- **Elder involvement** - Intergenerational knowledge
- **Holistic approach** - Family, culture, education, health
- **Location**: Remote/regional (Alice Springs, Central Australia)
- **Funding Status**: At-risk (underfunded despite proven success)

**Critical Insight**: Oochiumpa isn't "a program" - it's **Aboriginal-owned knowledge** about what healing looks like for young people disconnected from Country, kin, and culture.
