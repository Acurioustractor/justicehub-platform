## What Makes This Different

<div className="grid grid-cols-2 gap-6 my-8">
  <Callout type="success" title="Not Creating Dependency">
    Seed capital for <strong>community ownership</strong>, not ongoing program funding
  </Callout>

  <Callout type="success" title="Building Sovereignty">
    <strong>Permanent infrastructure</strong> owned by communities, not dependent services
  </Callout>

  <Callout type="success" title="Knowledge Valued">
    <strong>$1.8M annually</strong> (38% of Year 3 budget) to CORE communities + <strong>$1M worth of free support</strong> to NETWORK communities
  </Callout>

  <Callout type="success" title="Exponential Reach">
    <strong>29 communities</strong> reached by Year 3 through CORE/NETWORK model (vs 9 if all paid equally)
  </Callout>
</div>

## Potential Warm Community Partners

**CORE/NETWORK Model: Two-Tier Community Structure**

<Callout type="info" title="CORE Communities (Paid Partnerships)">
  <strong>9 by Year 3</strong> - Deep co-design partners receiving $180K annually. These communities have governance seats, participate in platform development, and receive 8-10 hours/week connector support.

  **Year 1 (5 CORE):**
  1. **Mparntwe (Alice Springs, NT)** - Oonchiumpa Consultancy
  2. **Minjerribah (North Stradbroke Island, QLD)** - MMEIC
  3. **Barranbinya (Bourke, NSW)** - Bourke Aboriginal Community
  4. **Yaama (Moree, NSW)** - Moree Aboriginal Community
  5. **Bwgcolman (Palm Island, QLD)** - Palm Island Community Company

  **Year 2 additions:** Darug Country (Mounty Yarns), Kalkadoon Country (BG Fit)

  **Year 3 additions:** 2 additional communities (identified through network effect)
</Callout>

<Callout type="success" title="NETWORK Communities (Free Support)">
  <strong>20 by Year 3</strong> - Receive free Community Connector support (2-4 hours/week) worth $40-50K annually. Get platform access, training, peer network, and can graduate to CORE in future years.

  **Value delivered:** Website development, grant writing support, impact frameworks, storytelling, event coordination, network building.

  **Graduation pathway:** NETWORK communities demonstrating strong engagement can become CORE partners in subsequent years.
</Callout>
