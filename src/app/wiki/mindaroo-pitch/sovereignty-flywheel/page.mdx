## Understanding the Visual

<Callout type="info" title="How the Flywheel Works">
  <div>

### The Six Core Sections

1. **THE PROBLEM** - Establishes urgency: 17x overrepresentation, knowledge hidden, grants consuming 30-40% of time
2. **THE SOLUTION** - JusticeHub serves ALL young people under Indigenous leadership with fair compensation ($180K/community)
3. **THE SOVEREIGNTY FLYWHEEL** - 6-step self-reinforcing cycle where each turn strengthens the next
4. **THE 3-YEAR JOURNEY** - CORE/NETWORK model shows exponential reach (5 ‚Üí 15 ‚Üí 29 communities)
5. **THE INVESTMENT** - $10.5M BASE scenario reaches 29 communities with 48% Indigenous workforce
6. **THE IMPACT** - Communities own platform by Year 5, sector transforms, funders see true scalability

### Key Insights

The CORE/NETWORK model is the breakthrough: 9 CORE communities (paid $180K) + 20 NETWORK communities (free $40-50K support) = 29 total reach for $3.33M instead of $5.22M. This creates:

- **5.8x reach multiplier** - Exponential impact within fixed budget
- **Community ownership** - Not dependency, sovereignty
- **Indigenous workforce** - 48% by Year 3 (16 of 33 staff)
- **Self-sustainability** - $120K revenue Year 3, full sustainability Year 8
