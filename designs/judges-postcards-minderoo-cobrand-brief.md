## Edits required in the .pen file

The co-brand is deliberately restrained. Minderoo is the evidence partner, not the subject of the postcards. The storytellers and Oonchiumpa remain the primary voices.

### Card 01 (thesis card)

Add a footer strip at the bottom of the face side. Contents:

- Left: existing JusticeHub / Oonchiumpa mark (unchanged)
- Right: small text block "Evidence partner: Minderoo Foundation" in IBM Plex Mono Medium, 9pt, `#5e5145` (warm gold-brown) on `#f8f1e6` background

Do NOT include the Minderoo logo on card 01. Text attribution only. This matches the pitch page's "named partner, not brand placement" framing.

### Cards 02–06 (storyteller cards)

**No co-brand mark on face side.** The face is the storyteller's portrait and voice — Minderoo's mark does not belong there.

### QR backs (all cards)

On the back of each card (where the QR code lives), add a single line of text below the QR:

```
Scan to view the full Empathy Ledger story at justicehub.com.au
Evidence partner: Minderoo Foundation
```

Formatting: IBM Plex Mono Regular, 8pt, `#7c6952`.

### Card 01 back specifically

Card 01 back carries the thesis ("This is not theoretical; it is already working"). Add the Minderoo attribution line BELOW the existing thesis statement, not above it. The thesis remains primary.

## What NOT to add

- No Minderoo logo (text attribution only)
- No Minderoo mark on storyteller face sides
- No "funded by" or "sponsored by" framing — the language is "evidence partner"
- No typography or colour changes to existing cards beyond the footer additions

## Language precision

The phrase is "**Evidence partner: Minderoo Foundation**" — not "sponsor", "funder", "supporter", "backed by", or "in partnership with". The specific language matters for the pitch reframe.
