### Lobbying Activity

The **25 November 2025 meeting** is notable: registered lobbyists Kevin Parer and Glen Elmes from **Australian Strategic Advisory** introduced **Colin Saltmere of Myuma Group** to discuss "youth justice and rehabilitation." This is the only explicitly disclosed lobbyist meeting in the 4-month period.

### Regional Focus

The diary reveals heavy regional engagement:
- **Far North Queensland / Cairns**: Namu Collective, Jabalbina Yalanji, Ohana for Youth, Holy Spirit College, Catholic Education Diocese of Cairns
- **Townsville**: Fresh Start Academy, Adam Baillie MP, Townsville Community Corrections
- **Mackay / Whitsunday**: Binga Birry Justice Group, Sarina Youth, Manawise, Reconcile Life, Mackay/Whitsunday/Isaac councils
- **Bundaberg / Wide Bay**: Bridges Health, Bundy Street Law
- **Toowoomba**: Anglicare SQ, OzChild, Goolburri
- **Gold Coast**: Skill360/BUSY Group graduation at Currumbin
- **Redcliffe**: Youth Insearch

### Meeting Pattern

- **Weekly Pre-cabinet Briefings**: DG of DYJVS + Commissioner QCS every Monday before Cabinet
- **Weekly Cabinet**: Every Monday
- **Fortnightly DG meetings**: Separate portfolio meetings with DG and Deputy DG
- **Victim-focused**: Multiple meetings with Victims Commissioner, Voice for Victims, Victim of Crime reps
- **Cross-ministerial**: Regular meetings with Attorney-General, Police Minister, Child Safety Minister on youth justice
