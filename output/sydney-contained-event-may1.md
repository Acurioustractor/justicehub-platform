## 6. KEY DATA TO SHOWCASE AT THE EVENT

### The Access Gap (NSW-specific)

- **$327M/year** NSW youth justice spend (ROGS 2025)
- **$939K** per person per year in detention
- **$1,708** cheapest effective community program (550:1 ratio)
- **$9.9M** "Breaking Cycle of Reoffending" grants — **$0 to ACCOs**
- **79.8%** of Aboriginal youth in detention are unsentenced
- **+34%** detention surge since March 2024 bail laws
- **154 mapped NSW programs** — 4 Proven, 16 Effective, 10 Indigenous-led
- **Philanthropic funders**: $115M in NSW, **71% through Indigenous-led orgs**
- **NSW Government**: **3.2%** to Indigenous orgs

### The Judges Event Link

- **55 judges** at Oonchiumpa mid-April
- JusticeHub evidence postcards — same data, reaching the judiciary
- "The data you fund → the evidence judges see → the system shifts"

### Mounty Yarns Growth Story

- 1 person with a camera → 20-person team
- $3.3M raised from 3+ funders
- Documentary: 100K+ views, TEDxSydney, NSW Children's Court Conference
- Backyard Campus designed by young people, endorsed by 6 Elders
- NSW's first Community-led Youth Justice Roundtable at UNSW (May 2025)
