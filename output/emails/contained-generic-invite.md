# CONTAINED — generic invite / spread-the-word email

Use for anyone: warm contacts, sector colleagues, potential amplifiers.
Personalise the first line per send. Subject options below.

**Subject:** Ten minutes inside the system they fund
**Softer alternative:** Want to walk through CONTAINED?

---

Hi [name],

You've probably heard me talk about CONTAINED. We built a children's prison cell inside a shipping container, true to the real dimensions, the bed, the observation windows, the walls kids scratch their names into. Three rooms. Ten minutes in each. The cell, the evidence, the alternative.

It's touring Australia now, and the next stop is Adelaide. I'd love your help getting the right people inside it.

Three ways, pick any:

**1. Walk through it yourself.**
Spots are limited and triaged by a human, not a form. Register your interest here:
justicehub.com.au/contained/eoi

**2. Nominate someone who makes the decisions.**
A premier, a minister, a judge, an editor, a CEO. Sixty seconds:
justicehub.com.au/contained#nominate
When you submit, we generate a share tile with their name stamped NOMINATED and your reason on it. Download it, post it, tag them. Every nomination goes on a public wall, and when CONTAINED reaches their city, the invitation arrives with your name behind it.

**3. Forward this email.**
One person who would never normally come to something like this. That's the person we built it for.

The numbers behind it, for when someone asks: locking up one child costs $1.33 million a year and 84% reoffend. Community-led alternatives cost about $75 a day. The evidence is in room two. The argument settles itself once you've stood in room one.

Thanks for carrying this further than I can on my own.

Ben

---

## Usage notes

- Cold contacts: swap the first line for "I'm writing because of the work you do in [X]."
- The stats are the campaign's standing figures ($1.33M = ROGS 2024-25 national average detention cost; 84% reoffending; ~$75/day alternatives).
- Pairs with the LinkedIn nomination-tile post; the tile generator lives at /api/contained/nomination-tile.
