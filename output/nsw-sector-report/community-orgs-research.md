# NSW Community Organisations & Justice Reinvestment Research

**Date:** 2026-03-29
**Source:** 20 web searches, 17 organisations profiled

---

## Justice Reinvestment Sites (5)

### 1. Maranguka (Bourke) — FLAGSHIP
- **KPMG validated:** 5:1 economic return ($3.1M impact vs $0.6M cost)
- 23% DV reduction, 42% custody days reduction, 38% juvenile offence reduction
- 31% rise in Year 12 retention
- Now independent from ALS NSW/ACT
- **In JusticeHub DB:** 11 programs mapped

### 2. MAYCS / Mounty Yarns (Mt Druitt) — GROWTH STORY
- From 1 person to 20-person team, $3.3M in grants
- NSW's first Community-led Youth Justice Roundtable at UNSW (May 2025)
- Documentary: 100K+ views, TEDxSydney, NSW Children's Court Conference
- Backyard Campus: yarning circle, gym, basketball, Aboriginal flag mural
- Connected to Daniel Daylight / Dusseldorp Forum
- **In JusticeHub DB:** 7 programs | **Empathy Ledger:** 14 transcripts, 1 story

### 3. Yuwaya Ngarra-li (Walgett)
- DEG partnership, 23+ years
- Court appearances down to 7.8%
- Won APPI grant Nov 2025
- Submitted to NSW Select Committee March 2026

### 4. Moree — MOST HEAVILY FUNDED HOTSPOT
- $13.4M + $2M+ additional
- Three ACCOs: Miyay Birray, SHAE Academy, Pius X AMS
- 90 young people per night in after-hours activities

### 5. Dubbo — MEASURABLE RESULTS
- Youth crime halved: 317 incidents (2024) to 159 (2025)
- Oyster Tribe delivering SAY program
