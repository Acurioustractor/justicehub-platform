## Tier B: YJ-Adjacent

- **Myer Foundation** — $2.17M anniversary grants, emphasis on Indigenous & disadvantaged youth. No specific YJ grants.
- **Tim Fairfax** — $8.1M/yr to 43 orgs, QLD/NT focus. Resilience Stream $3.7M.
- **Origin Foundation** — 20%+ to Indigenous education. Grant King Scholarship.
- **Macquarie Group** — ~$2.2M AU, Brotherhood of St Laurence Compass tool.
- **Gandel** — invitation-only until Feb 2026. Known: Ganbina (Aboriginal youth dev).
- **Perpetual IMPACT** — $32M/yr, **2025 theme: "Youth at Risk"** (applications Oct-Dec).

## Key Insights
1. **Ian Potter** is biggest untapped opportunity — $35M/yr, public grants DB, funds Indigenous ACCOs
2. **Lotterywest** is a WA powerhouse — $1.8M to Binar Futures alone
3. **Perpetual's 2025 "Youth at Risk"** theme is time-sensitive (Oct-Dec applications)
4. **CAGES Foundation** co-funding Maranguka was previously undocumented
5. JR philanthropy field is tiny — only PRF, Dusseldorp, VFFF explicitly fund JR. Partnership opportunity for JusticeHub.
