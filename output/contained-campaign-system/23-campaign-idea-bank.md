## The frame before the ideas

CONTAINED is the emotional front door. JusticeHub is the public evidence and coordination layer that shows what works instead. Empathy Ledger holds consent, story, and community authority. The Australian Living Map of Alternatives is the evidence intelligence engine that keeps the whole thing current. Every idea here moves a person from the door, through the evidence, toward backing what works. Never start and end inside detention. Always arrive at the alternative.

The architecture this bank serves has three pressures, all pointing at one week.

The booking funnel is empty. Pipeline B (CONTAINED Adelaide 2026) holds zero real demand ten days out. Filling it is the launch-week priority, and most of the top ideas exist to feed it.

The warmest people are the least informed. The fourteen inbound contacts who asked about the container have not been told it is happening. The fourteen KEEP funders have had no personal touch. The warmth is real and the silence is the problem.

The headline mechanic has no path to its target. Nomination is the campaign's loudest move, yet the nominated decision-maker never becomes a record and never gets reached except by hand. The ladder breaks at its most important rung.

So the ideas sort into four jobs: fill Pipeline B, inform the dark segments, make the nomination mechanic actually reach someone, and turn launch week into ripples that outlive the container.

### The escalation ladder (Beautiful Trouble), every idea maps to a rung

Witness → Nominate → Back → Share → Host → Fund → Document.

A person should be able to find their next rung on every surface. The witness books a slot. The witness who walked through nominates a decision-maker. The nominator shares the question. The sharer hosts a venue. The host introduces a funder. The funder documents the next city. The bank below is organised first by priority, then tagged by rung so no surface leaves a person without a next step.
