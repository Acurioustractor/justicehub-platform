Language Rules

Do / Don't

Always Use

  • System-impacted young people
  • Community-led alternatives
  • Justice infrastructure
  • What works — lead with solutions
  • Evidence-based — every claim sourced
  • The ask is concrete — back what works

Never Use

  • "Offenders" or "juvenile offenders"
  • "At-risk" without qualifying who defined the risk
  • "Tough on crime" framing, even to critique
  • AI-generated photorealistic images of people
  • Sensationalised language about young people
  • "Justice tech" or "justice platform"
