# Justice Reinvestment Census — Australia, June 2026

Generated: 2026-06-10
Purpose: a verified census of justice reinvestment initiatives across Australia, reconciled against the 55 records JusticeHub already holds. The authoritative spine is the Attorney-General's Department list of Commonwealth-funded initiatives. State, philanthropic and advocacy-stage sites are added where a source confirms them.

Method note: the AG page publishes 28 funded initiatives (26 National Program, 2 Central Australia Program) and names 27 as currently active, each with grantee organisation and place. The Department does not publish a clean round 1 / round 2 split per site, so funding source is recorded as "NJRP (Commonwealth)" or "Central Australia Program (Commonwealth)" rather than an invented round number. State, ACT and advocacy sites carry their own source.
