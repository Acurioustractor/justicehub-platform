# CONTAINED Launch Runbook

Date: March 12, 2026

## Core Line

`CONTAINED shows what youth detention feels like. JusticeHub shows what works instead.`

## Public Story

- `CONTAINED` is the emotional front door.
- `JusticeHub` is the public evidence and coordination layer.
- `Empathy Ledger` protects consent, lived experience, and community authority.
- `ALMA` keeps the evidence base live and accountable.

## Rollout Today

1. Push `/contained` as the campaign front door.
2. Push `/contained/act` as the action page for email, SMS, and social forwarding.
3. Push `/contained/stories` as the proof-of-voice surface.
4. Push `/contained/nominations` as the public pressure surface.
5. Push `/contained/tour/social` as the share kit for partners and media.
6. Use Mount Druitt, April 25, 2026 as the anchor date across every public mention.

## Must-Hit Messages

- One shipping container. Three rooms. Thirty minutes.
- Youth detention is expensive, harmful, and racially unequal.
- Community-led alternatives already exist.
- The ask is not abstract reform. The ask is to back what works.

## Launch Sequence

1. Publish the main page and verify the Mount Druitt countdown.
2. Confirm nominations, backers, reactions, and media APIs are returning public data.
3. Send partner outreach using `/contained/act` templates.
4. Give all partners the `/contained/tour/social` link and tell them to use the same core line.
5. Send media directly to `/contained`, not to a generic homepage.
6. Use `/justice-funding?state=NSW` as the follow-through link when people ask where money currently goes.

## Parallel Research Tracks

### Campaign Ops Agents

- Watch nominations volume by category and identify missing sectors.
- Watch partner shares and media pickups.
- Recommend next outreach pushes by state and stop.

### Evidence Agents

- Find new youth justice programs, evaluations, and budget items.
- Link claims on the campaign pages back to public evidence.
- Keep ALMA stats current before each tour stop.

### Ranking Agents

- Rank for trust, source quality, community authority, freshness, and usefulness.
- Penalise weak sourcing, duplicated claims, and extractive framing.

### Guardrails

- No automated profiling of vulnerable people.
- No story ingestion without explicit consent boundaries.
- Indigenous and community-led authority must outrank institutional prestige.
- Human review stays in the loop for anything public-facing.

## Success Today

- The first page people land on is coherent.
- The first line people repeat is coherent.
- The first action they take is obvious.
- The evidence path behind the campaign is visible.
