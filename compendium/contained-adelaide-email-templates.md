Hi [Name],

CONTAINED lands in Adelaide 22 to 26 June: a three-room container experience about youth detention, built and fronted by young people.

Two things I would love to talk through with you this week.

**Paid host roles.** We are employing young people as greeters and hosts: welcoming visitors, holding the timing, being the first human face of the experience. Shifts are paid, supervised, with breaks and a support person on site. If young people in your network would want this, we ask them one question to start: why do you want to do it, and what would you like to earn?

**Consent pathways.** Nothing about any young person enters this experience without their say-so, on their terms, with their people around them. Some young people may want their story, artwork or voice in the rooms. We would only ever do that through you, at their pace, under Empathy Ledger consent protocols, and it is completely fine if the answer is no.

Could we grab 20 minutes on the phone this week? I can come to you.
