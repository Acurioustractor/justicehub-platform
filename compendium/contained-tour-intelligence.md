> "Australia spends $1.55 million per child per year in detention. 84% reoffend. Community programs cost $75 a day with an 88% success rate. CONTAINED doesn't ask you to believe this. It shows you."

### By Audience

**For young people:** "This was designed by young people who have been through the system. Room 1 is their room."

**For community orgs:** "Room 3 is yours. It changes at every tour stop. The community that hosts the container gets to tell their own story to decision-makers who would never otherwise hear it."

**For funders:** "You're not funding a container. You're funding permanent infrastructure — 876 verified alternatives, $72B in funding tracked, 18,000+ organisations mapped. CONTAINED is the front door."

**For politicians:** "We're showing your own government's data. Productivity Commission, ROGS, AIHW. The numbers are yours. We just put them in a room."

**For media:** "A shipping container is touring Australia. Inside: three rooms, thirty minutes, and the evidence that could end youth detention as we know it."

### Content Principles
1. **Real photos only** — Empathy Ledger, consent-first, never AI-generated
2. **Every number has a source** — ROGS, Productivity Commission, AIHW, community evaluations
3. **Community authority** — Indigenous communities decide how their stories are told
4. **One ask per touchpoint** — don't overwhelm. Experience it / Nominate / Back the tour
5. **The data does the work** — $4,250/day vs $75/day speaks louder than any argument
