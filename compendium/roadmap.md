## 2026 Focus Areas

### 1. Deepen ALMA Alignment
- Ensure every insight respects cultural sovereignty
- Avoid profiling — track system-level signals, not individuals
- Strengthen ethical guardrails for AI research features

### 2. Lead Australian Justice Innovation
- Position JusticeHub as next-generation justice infrastructure
- Build evidence base from community-led programs (Operation Luna, Oonchiumpa)
- International learning partnerships (Diagrama)

### 3. Lived-Experience Leadership Pathways
- Support pathways for system-impacted youth into advocacy roles
- Brodie Germaine model as template for supported leadership

### 4. Strengthen Storytelling Connection
- Deeper integration with Empathy Ledger
- More consented vignettes from justice work
- Better evidence-to-story pipeline

### 5. Platform Health
- Investigate and resolve health score issues
- Deployment stability
- Database optimisation across 3 Supabase instances
