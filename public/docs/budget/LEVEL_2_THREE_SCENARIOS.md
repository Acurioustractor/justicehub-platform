# Level 2: Three Investment Scenarios
## Strategic Options for Mindaroo Foundation

**Investment Philosophy:** Each scenario represents a viable approach with different levels of ambition across three strategic pillars:
1. **Community Engagement** - Depth and breadth of community partnerships
2. **Development** - Platform sophistication and Indigenous capacity building
3. **Communications & Content** - Scale and quality of storytelling and sector engagement

---

## THREE-SCENARIO OVERVIEW

| Scenario | 3-Year Total | Philosophy | Risk Level |
|----------|--------------|------------|------------|
| **LEAN** | **$5.5M - $6.5M** | Prove the model with minimal viable engagement | Lower risk, slower impact |
| **BASE** | **$7.0M - $8.5M** | Solid foundation for sustainable scale | Balanced risk/impact |
| **UPPER** | **$9.0M - $11.0M** | Comprehensive delivery with optimal community compensation | Higher investment, faster systems change |

### **Recommend BASE Scenario: $6.5M - $7.5M over 3 years**

✅ **Community Engagement:** 6 communities with fair compensation ($40-50K per community per 6 months)
✅ **Development:** Quality platform + 4 Indigenous developers
✅ **Communications:** Meaningful content and sector engagement
✅ **Operations:** 5 FTE team that can actually deliver
✅ **Risk Balance:** Proven model with sustainable scale
✅ **Value for Money:** Every dollar drives meaningful impact

**When to choose LEAN ($4.5M-$5.5M):**
- Mindaroo wants to test the model first
- Lower risk tolerance
- Phased decision-making after Year 1
- Budget constraints prioritize other portfolios

**When to choose UPPER ($8.5M-$10.0M):**
- Mindaroo wants comprehensive systems change
- High confidence in model and team
- Want strong international dimension from Day 1
- Deeper community compensation is priority
- Faster path to sustainability
