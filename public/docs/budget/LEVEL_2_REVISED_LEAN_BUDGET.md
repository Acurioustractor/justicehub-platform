### COMMUNITY PAYMENTS - PAY-PER-ENGAGEMENT MODEL

**Philosophy:** Pay for specific engagements, content, and time rather than blanket annual payments. This ensures value-for-money while respecting Indigenous knowledge.

| **Content Creation Payments** | $8K | $48K | 3 videos × $2K + 5 stories × $400 | Per-piece payment. Is $2K per video fair? |
| **Meeting Attendance Fees** | $3K | $18K | 6 meetings × $500 per meeting (3-4 community members) | Covers time + travel. Adequate? |

**Is $40K per community for 6 months reasonable?** Scales to ~$80K/year in Years 2-3 if engagement is similar.

**Key principle:** Communities get paid when they actively engage, not as blanket payment. More work = more payment.
