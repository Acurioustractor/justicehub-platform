The partnership, in three movements

The Three Circles

Each circle feeds the next. None is the product. The holding of all three for three years is.

Concentric, not sequential. The centre is the evidence. The ring is the practice. The edge is the artefact. Remove one and the method collapses.

Circle One · The Centre

The Living Brain

Everything on Australian youth justice. Queryable, updating, community-governed. The national evidence backbone of community-led alternatives, held under Indigenous data sovereignty with a 12-gate consent chain.
