The Opportunity

CONTAINED is not a campaign looking for an audience. The audience is already here — asking where, when, and how they can help. The demand outstrips our capacity to deliver.

Five cities are confirmed. Eight more are requesting. Two state MPs have publicly asked for tour dates. A former Chief Medical Officer called the current system "delusional." A QLD MP asked when it's coming. An ACT Inspector of Custodial Services said government is ready for a new model.

Behind the Container sits ALMA — 981 verified community alternatives to youth detention, mapped nationally with cost data. And JusticeHub — tracking $71M+ in youth justice funding across every state, showing where money goes vs where evidence says it should.

Each tour stop costs $30–50K. Each one generates measurable community engagement, coalition formation, and policy pressure. The infrastructure exists. The demand is proven. The coalition is forming. We need partners who want to accelerate it.

Join the Coalition

CONTAINED starts in Sydney end of April 2026. Then Adelaide. Then Perth. Then wherever communities are ready to stop managing this system and start replacing it.
