## üìä Recommended Budget Scenario for Mindaroo

Based on review:

**‚úÖ RECOMMENDATION: BASE / Level 2 / Foundational**

**Amount:** **$10.5M over 3 years**
- Year 1: $3.1M
- Year 2: $3.6M
- Year 3: $3.8M (net of $60K revenue)

**Range for flexibility:** $9.5M - $13.0M (allows negotiation within bounds)

**Rationale:**

1. **Meaningful systems change** - LEAN is too small to demonstrate full model; UPPER may be too ambitious for Mindaroo's risk appetite
2. **6 communities** provides national representation without overextending
3. **Quality platform** that's community-ready, not just functional MVP
4. **Realistic capacity building** - 2‚Üí4 Indigenous developers is achievable
5. **Revenue pilot** demonstrates sustainability pathway
6. **International connections** (NZ + Canada delegations) bring global best practice
7. **Cost per community ($1.75M over 3 years)** is fair for deep partnership, not token engagement
8. **Direct community investment ($3.52M)** shows proper valuation of Indigenous knowledge
9. **Clear path to ownership** - governance structures operational by Year 3, transfer by Year 5

**This is the "Goldilocks" scenario** - not too small to prove the model, not so large it's overwhelming. It's the scenario that best demonstrates transformational philanthropy (building permanent community infrastructure) vs transactional funding (ongoing program support).
