## üé® Formatting Recommendations

### Typography
- **Font:** Clean, professional
  - Sans-serif: Helvetica, Arial, Calibri
  - Serif: Georgia, Garamond, Times New Roman
- **Size:**
  - Name: 16-18pt
  - Section headers: 12-13pt
  - Body: 10-11pt
- **Color:** Black or very dark gray (#1a1a1a)
- **Headers:** Bold or semi-bold

### Layout
- **Margins:** 0.75-1 inch all sides
- **Line spacing:** 1.1-1.2
- **Section spacing:** Extra space between sections (14-16pt)
- **Alignment:** Left-aligned (easier to read than justified)
- **Page numbers:** Bottom right or center (except page 1)

### Visual Hierarchy
1. **Name** - Largest, bold
2. **Title/Tagline** - Medium, may use color accent
3. **Contact info** - Small, may use icons
4. **Section headers** - Bold, consistent size
5. **Job titles** - Bold or semi-bold
6. **Dates/Locations** - Italics or gray
7. **Body text** - Regular weight

### Professional Polish
- Consistent bullet point style (‚Ä¢ or ‚Äì)
- Consistent date format (Month Year or MM/YYYY)
- No widows/orphans (single lines at top/bottom of pages)
- Professional header on subsequent pages (Name | CV | Page X)
- White space for readability (don't cram too much)
