## üé® DESIGN NOTES FOR ALL VISUALS

### Color Palette Guidelines
- **Primary:** Indigenous earth tones (ochre #D4926A, deep red #8B3A3A, warm brown #6B4423)
- **Secondary:** Cool confidence (deep blue #2C5F7C, forest green #3A6B5C)
- **Accents:** Hope and growth (bright green #7CB342, optimistic blue #42A5F5)
- **Urgency:** Warm warnings (amber #FFA726, cautious orange #FF7043)

### Typography Principles
- **Headings:** Bold, clear, scannable
- **Body:** Readable at distance (presentations)
- **Callouts:** Highlighted, memorable
- **Numbers:** Large, prominent (investment figures, percentages)

### Visual Hierarchy
1. **The Ask:** Always prominent ($10.5M, BASE Scenario)
2. **The Impact:** Always visible (communities, developers, sovereignty)
3. **The Evidence:** Always present (cost reduction, revenue pathway)
4. **The Difference:** Always clear (community ownership, not dependency)

### Cultural Sensitivity
- Use respectful Indigenous design elements where appropriate
- Avoid stereotypical symbols (boomerangs, dot painting unless authentic)
- Consult First Nations designers for final visual identity
- Ensure all visuals honor community sovereignty principle
